Bed Levelling Mount Remix by D. Pantazis

1. Bed levelling blocks reconfigured with annotations for adjusting the nozzle / bed gap. 

2. Graduated thumb wheel to aid in adjusting the bed. 

3. Test flat of the recessed test to tweak printer settings. 

4. Spring keeper centering washer for top of spring. To center and align the spring to the M5 stud/nut. 

Blocks attach to 20x20 frame with M5x20 cap head screws. 

Levelling studs for bed attachment M5x60 double nutted to heat spreader plate.  

Springs are 1/2" OD x 1" min length.
